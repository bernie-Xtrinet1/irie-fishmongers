# COLD CHAIN REQUIREMENTS
# Irie Fishmongers Marketplace

Version: 1.0

---

# OBJECTIVE

Maintain safe seafood temperatures from catch to delivery.

No product may break cold-chain requirements.

---

# COLD CHAIN DEFINITION

Cold Chain:

Catch

→ Landing

→ Storage

→ Fulfillment

→ Transport

→ Delivery

Every stage must be monitored.

---

# TEMPERATURE REQUIREMENTS

Fresh Seafood

0°C to 4°C

Frozen Seafood

-18°C or below

Thresholds configurable.

---

# TEMPERATURE CHECKPOINTS

Required at:

- Catch
- Landing Site
- Storage Entry
- Storage Exit
- Packing
- Dispatch
- Driver Pickup
- Delivery

No checkpoint may be skipped.

---

# VIOLATION RULES

Generate alerts when:

Temperature exceeds threshold.

Severity:

Warning

Critical

Emergency

# PRODUCT STATUS

Normal

Warning

---

# QUARANTINE RULES

Products may be quarantined when:

Temperature abuse occurs.

Inspection fails.

Food safety concerns exist.

Quarantined inventory cannot be sold.

---

# DELIVERY VALIDATION

Temperature must be verified:

Before dispatch

At pickup

At delivery
