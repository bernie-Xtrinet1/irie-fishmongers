# IrieFishmongers Glossary

Version: 1.0

---

# Customer

A user who purchases seafood products through the platform.

Examples:

- Household buyer
- Restaurant
- Hotel
- Caterer
- Wholesale buyer

---

# Vendor

An approved seafood supplier selling products through the marketplace.

Examples:

- Fisherman
- Fish Market
- Seafood Distributor
- Seafood Wholesaler

---

# Driver

A delivery partner responsible for collecting and delivering orders.

---

# Administrator

A platform operator responsible for governance and oversight.

---

# Product

A seafood item available for purchase.

Examples:

- Snapper
- Lobster
- Shrimp
- Conch

---

# Inventory

The available quantity of a product a vendor can sell.

Inventory cannot be negative.

---

# Cart

Temporary collection of products selected by a customer.

---

# Master Order

The single order visible to the customer.

May contain products supplied by multiple vendors.

Example:

Order #1001

Contains:

Vendor A = 20 lbs Snapper
Vendor B = 15 lbs Snapper

Customer sees:

One order

---

# Vendor Order

A vendor-specific portion of a master order.

Created automatically when multi-vendor fulfillment occurs.

---

# Multi-Vendor Fulfillment

Process where multiple vendors contribute inventory to satisfy one customer order.

---

# Delivery Consolidation

Process of combining pickups from multiple vendors into a single customer delivery.

Goal:

- Lower delivery cost
- Reduce travel distance
- Improve efficiency

---

# Service Area

Geographical region a vendor is willing to serve.

---

# Delivery Zone

A defined area used for delivery calculations and pricing.

---

# Allocation Engine

System responsible for selecting vendors to fulfill an order.

Selection criteria:

- Distance
- Inventory
- Vendor rating
- Delivery cost

---

# Settlement

Distribution of collected funds to vendors after successful order processing.

---

# Platform Fee

Commission retained by IrieFishmongers.

---

# Delivery Fee

Fee charged for transportation services.

---

# Refund

Return of customer funds according to approved refund policies.

---

# Proof Of Delivery

Evidence that an order was successfully delivered.

Examples:

- Signature
- Photograph

---

# Vendor Rating

Performance score assigned to vendors.

Factors:

- Reviews
- Fulfillment rate
- Delivery performance

---

# Driver Assignment

Process of allocating a driver to a delivery.

---

# Pending Order

Order submitted but not yet accepted.

---

# Accepted Order

Vendor has committed inventory.

---

# Preparing Order

Products are being cleaned, packed, or prepared.

---

# Ready For Pickup

Order is available for collection.

---

# In Transit

Order is currently being delivered.

---

# Delivered

Order successfully completed.

---

# Cancelled

Order terminated before completion.

---

# Settlement Cycle

Frequency of vendor payments.

Examples:

- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
