# Location-Aware Inventory Allocation

The platform shall prioritize vendor selection based on:

1. Delivery feasibility
2. Distance from customer
3. Inventory availability
4. Vendor rating
5. Delivery cost optimization

Inventory availability alone shall not determine allocation.

---

# Vendor Selection Process

When a customer submits an order:

Step 1

Identify delivery address.

Step 2

Calculate vendor distance from delivery address.

Step 3

Rank vendors by:

- Service area eligibility
- Distance
