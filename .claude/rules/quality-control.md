# Quality Control

## Purpose

Identify unsafe seafood before delivery.

## Vendor Inspection Checklist

Vendor must verify:

- Eyes clear
- Gills bright red
- Flesh firm
- No unusual odor
- Proper packaging
- Correct temperature

## Driver Inspection Checklist

Driver shall verify:

- Packaging intact
- No leakage
- Ice present
- Temperature acceptable

## Quality Score

Each vendor shall have:

Quality Score = 0 - 100

Factors:

- Complaints
- Spoilage reports
- Temperature breaches
- Inspection failures

## Quality Incidents

Examples:

- Spoiled Fish
- Damaged Packaging
- Temperature Violation
- Wrong Product

## Escalation Rules

Three major incidents in 30 days:

Vendor Status = Under Review

Five major incidents in 90 days:

Vendor Status = Suspended
