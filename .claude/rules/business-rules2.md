# Customer Cancellation Policy

Customers may cancel orders without penalty only while the order status is:

- Pending

If payment was made online, a full refund is issued automatically.

---

Customers may not cancel orders once the order status is:

- Accepted
- Preparing
- Ready For Pickup
- Assigned To Driver
- In Transit
- Delivered

At this stage the seafood has been reserved, prepared, packed, or dispatched.

---

# Refund Policy

Eligible for Full Refund:

- Vendor rejects order
- Product unavailable
- Payment processing error
- Delivery cannot be completed due to vendor fault

Eligible for Partial Refund:

- Administrator-approved exceptional circumstances

Not Eligible for Refund:

- Customer changes mind after vendor acceptance
- Customer unavailable during delivery
- Incorrect delivery address supplied by customer
- Customer refuses delivery of correctly fulfilled order

---

# Failed Delivery Policy

If delivery fails because of customer actions:

- Customer not present
- Incorrect contact information
- Incorrect address
- Refusal of delivery

The order is considered fulfilled.

No refund will be issued.

A re-delivery fee may be charged if re-delivery is possible.

---

# Vendor Protection

Vendors must receive payment for:

- Accepted orders
- Prepared orders
- Dispatched orders

Customer cancellations after acceptance must not negatively impact vendor earnings.

---

# Perishable Goods Rule

Seafood products are classified as perishable goods.

Once an order enters the Preparing stage:

- Ownership of prepared inventory is allocated to the customer.
- Inventory cannot be returned to active stock.
- Standard cooling-off cancellation rights do not apply.
