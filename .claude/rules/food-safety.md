# Food Safety Rules

## Purpose

Ensure all seafood products sold through the platform meet food safety standards from catch to customer delivery.

## Objectives

- Maintain seafood quality.
- Prevent spoilage.
- Protect customers.
- Ensure regulatory compliance.
- Maintain traceability.

## Core Principles

Every seafood item must:

- Be traceable to a vendor.
- Be traceable to a catch batch.
- Have temperature records.
- Have quality inspection records.
- Have delivery records.

## Product Traceability

Every seafood batch shall have:

- Batch ID
- Vendor ID
- Catch Date
- Catch Location
- Species
- Weight
- Storage Temperature

Example:

BATCH-2027-000123

## Mandatory Records

The platform shall store:

- Catch information
- Inventory movements
- Temperature logs
- Delivery records
- Customer complaints
- Quality incidents

## Product Recall

If a seafood batch is deemed unsafe:

- Mark batch as RECALL_REQUIRED
- Prevent future sales
- Notify affected customers
- Notify operations staff

## Vendor Responsibility

Vendors are responsible for:

- Safe storage
- Accurate inventory
- Quality verification
- Batch registration

Repeated violations may result in:

- Warning
- Suspension
- Account termination
