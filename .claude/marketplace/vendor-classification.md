# VENDOR CLASSIFICATION

# Irie Fishmongers Marketplace

Version: 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

Define vendor categories, permissions, compliance requirements,
and marketplace capabilities.

---

# VENDOR TIERS

Tier 1

Community Fisher

Tier 2

Verified Vendor

Tier 3

Commercial Supplier

Tier 4

Enterprise Supplier

---

# COMMUNITY FISHER

Examples:

- Individual Fisher
- Beach Vendor
- Fish Ground Seller
- Canoe Fisher

Required:

- Government ID
- Phone Verification
- Address
- Fishing Area
- Food Safety Agreement

Not Required:

- Tax Compliance Certificate
- Insurance Certificate
- Business Registration

Capabilities:

- Retail Sales
- Marketplace Listings
- Local Deliveries

Restrictions:

- No Institutional Contracts
- No Wholesale Marketplace
- Daily Sales Limits

---

# VERIFIED VENDOR

Required:

- Government ID
- Business Registration
- Bank Account

Optional:

- Tax Compliance
- Insurance

Capabilities:

- Retail Sales
- Promotions
- Enhanced Search Ranking

---

# COMMERCIAL SUPPLIER

Required:

- Business Registration
- Tax Compliance
- Insurance
- Food Safety Documentation

Capabilities:

- Wholesale
- Restaurants
- Hotels
- Bulk Orders

---

# ENTERPRISE SUPPLIER

Required:

- Full Compliance Documentation
- Regulatory Certifications
- Insurance
- Quality Management Procedures

Capabilities:

- National Supply
- Institutional Contracts
- API Integrations

---

# TIER UPGRADE RULES

Vendor may request upgrade.

Admin approval required.

Required documents must be verified.

---

# TIER DOWNGRADE RULES

May occur because of:

- Compliance Failures
- Expired Documents
- Food Safety Violations
- Fraud

---

# CLAUDE EXECUTION RULE

Vendor requirements must be dynamically driven by tier configuration.
