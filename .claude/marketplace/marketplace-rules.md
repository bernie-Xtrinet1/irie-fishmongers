# MARKETPLACE RULES

Version: 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

Define marketplace operating policies.

---

# PRODUCT RULES

Only approved seafood products may be sold.

Products must include:

- Product Name
- Species
- Weight
- Price
- Catch Date
- Vendor

---

# FOOD SAFETY RULES

Products must comply with:

Food Safety

Traceability

Cold Chain

Quality Control

---

# VENDOR RULES

Vendors must maintain:

Accurate Inventory

Accurate Product Information

Compliance Documentation

---

# ORDER RULES

Orders may not be fulfilled without:

Inventory Availability

Vendor Acceptance

Compliance Validation

---

# DELIVERY RULES

Orders must be delivered within approved temperature ranges.

Temperature records required.

---

# CUSTOMER PROTECTION

Customers may:

Report Issues

Request Refunds

Leave Ratings

---

# PROHIBITED ACTIVITY

Fraud

Misrepresentation

Counterfeit Products

Food Safety Violations

Manipulation of Ratings

---

# ENFORCEMENT

Warning

Suspension

Tier Downgrade

Account Termination

---

# CLAUDE EXECUTION RULE

Marketplace policies must be enforceable by system workflows.
