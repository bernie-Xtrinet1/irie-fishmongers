# VENDOR ONBOARDING

Version: 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

Define onboarding workflow for all vendor types.

---

# STEP 1

Select Vendor Type

Options:

- Community Fisher
- Verified Vendor
- Commercial Supplier
- Enterprise Supplier

---

# STEP 2

Account Creation

Required:

- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Password

---

# STEP 3

Profile Setup

Capture:

- Business Name
- Address
- Operating Area
- Parish
- Fishing Location

---

# STEP 4

Document Submission

Requirements depend on vendor tier.

Community Fisher:

- Government ID

Verified Vendor:

- Government ID
- Business Registration

Commercial Supplier:

- Business Registration
- Tax Compliance
- Insurance

Enterprise Supplier:

- Full Documentation Package

---

# STEP 5

Food Safety Agreement

Vendor must accept:

- Seafood Safety Rules
- Traceability Requirements
- Cold Chain Requirements

---

# STEP 6

Verification

Submit for Review.

Status:

Pending

---

# STEP 7

Approval

Admin reviews submission.

Status:

Approved
Rejected
More Information Required

---

# STEP 8

Marketplace Activation

Vendor profile becomes visible.

Listings may be created.

---

# CLAUDE EXECUTION RULE

Onboarding forms must adapt dynamically based on vendor tier.
