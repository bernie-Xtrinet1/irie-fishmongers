# Cold Chain Requirements

## Objective

Ensure seafood remains within safe temperature ranges throughout fulfillment.

## Temperature Limits

Fresh Seafood

0°C - 4°C

Frozen Seafood

-18°C or colder

## Monitoring Frequency

Manual Monitoring

- Pickup
- Delivery
- Hub Transfer

IoT Monitoring

Every 5 minutes

## Vehicle Requirements

Refrigerated vehicles must support:

- Temperature monitoring
- GPS tracking
- Alert notifications

## Alert Thresholds

Warning

Temperature > 4°C for 15 minutes

Critical

Temperature > 4°C for 30 minutes

## Required Actions

Warning:

- Notify Driver

Critical:

- Notify Operations
- Create Incident
- Review Product Safety

## Compliance Dashboard

Operations shall view:

- Active temperature breaches
- Vehicle temperatures
- Vendor violations
- Product incidents
