# Food Safety Compliance

## Purpose

Define regulatory and operational food safety requirements.

## Compliance Objectives

The platform shall:

- Maintain product traceability.
- Record food handling events.
- Record delivery chain events.
- Record temperature compliance.

## Traceability Requirements

Every seafood item must be linked to:

- Vendor
- Batch
- Order
- Driver
- Customer

## Data Retention

The platform shall retain:

- Order records
- Delivery records
- Temperature records
- Quality incidents

Minimum retention:

7 years

## Audit Support

The platform shall generate:

- Batch history reports
- Temperature history reports
- Vendor quality reports
- Delivery chain reports
